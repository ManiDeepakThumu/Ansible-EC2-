Why join Webgains

Flexible approach to office and homeworking

We understand how important a good work-life balance is, and as a result, have adopted a flexible approach to home/office working. You can even bring your furry friends into your Webgains office with you if you'd like.

Learning, Development and Progression

We're committed to developing and encouraging our staff. Whether this be through our quarterly training weeks, or through our annual £2000 Professional Training Loan. Want to progress in your personal life? Thinking about learning to drive or taking your puppy to puppy classes? No worries, we’ve got you covered with our £2000 Personal Loan too.

International Office Exchange

Change your dream of working abroad into a reality with our International Office Exchange. We encourage our teams to work from our different global offices, experience the culture and get to know our global teams. You can even enroll in our free Language classes to help you along the way.

Mental Health and Wellbeing, and Equalities, Diversity and Inclusion

We are very passionate about these topics and have different internal groups working to ensure we are an inclusive employer. You will have access to free counselling sessions, as well as our amazing Mental Health First Aiders.

N.B. The benefits above are just an example of what is on offer at Webgains. They may vary from one country to another.
